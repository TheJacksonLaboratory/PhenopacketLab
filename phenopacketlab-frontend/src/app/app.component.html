Phenopacket Lab

PhenopacketLab is a single-page application for creating and editing phenopackets. It provides a wizard to build phenopackets from scratch and an editor for updating existing phenopackets.

You can use your ORCiD profile to login to the application and save your phenopackets. If you do not want to login, you can still use the application anonymously. However, ensure you download the phenopackets - the data will not be saved, and you will lose the phenopackets if you close or refresh your browser tab.

The Phenopacket Schema is an emerging GLobal Alliance for Genomics and Health (GA4GH) standard for sharing disease and phenotype information that characterizes an individual person, linking that individual to detailed phenotypic descriptions, genetic information, diagnoses, and treatments.

The Phenopacket Schema, together with other GA4GH data and technical standards, will enable data exchange and provide a foundation for the computational analysis of disease and phenotype information to improve our ability to diagnose and conduct research on all types of disorders, including cancer and rare diseases. Phenopackets can be used for a growing number of computational tasks, including serving as input for genomic analysis software such as such as Exomiser, LIRICAL, SvAnna.
